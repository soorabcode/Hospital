Our Mission & Vision

At Soorab Clinic, we are dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services with compassion, integrity, and innovation. Our mission is to improve the health and well-being of our community through comprehensive medical care, patient education, and preventive health initiatives.

Since our founding in 2000, we have grown from a small family practice to a premier multi-specialty medical center, serving thousands of patients with the highest standards of care. Our vision is to be the healthcare provider of choice for families in our community, recognized for clinical excellence and patient-centered care.

General Questions

Soorab Clinic offers comprehensive healthcare services including cardiology, neurology, pediatrics, orthopedics, general medicine, and emergency care. We also provide preventive health screenings, wellness programs, and specialized diagnostic services.

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans including Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, and Medicare. We also offer self-pay options and flexible payment plans. Please contact our billing department for specific insurance inquiries.

Our regular operating hours are Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We provide 24/7 emergency services for urgent medical needs. Please call ahead for weekend and holiday appointments.

Appointments

You can book an appointment through our website using the online booking form, by calling our appointment desk at (123) 456-7890, or by visiting our clinic in person. We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance for non-urgent appointments.

For your first appointment, please bring your insurance card, photo ID, a list of current medications, any relevant medical records or test results, and completed new patient forms (available for download on our website). Arrive 15 minutes early to complete any remaining paperwork.

Medical Services

Yes, we offer virtual consultations for follow-up appointments, medication management, and certain types of consultations. Telemedicine services are available through our secure patient portal. Not all conditions are suitable for virtual care, so please discuss with your provider.

Your test results will be available through our secure patient portal within 3-5 business days for most tests. Your provider will contact you if any results require immediate attention. You can also call our office to request results after they have been reviewed by your physician.
